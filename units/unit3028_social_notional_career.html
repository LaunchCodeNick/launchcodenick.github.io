Social Exercise - Programming Careers

  • 30 minutes
  • Group Exercise
  • Exercise Objectives
    • Share why you decided to take this course
    • Share what you know or imagine about programming careers

Group Activity

For this exercise, place yourself in small groups of 3-4 people.

In your small group, take 15 minutes to share your thoughts on the following questions:

Why did you take this course? What do you know about programming careers? Do you know anyone who works in the field? Whether you know much yet or not, what do you think being a programmer is like? Whatâ€™s a typical day like? How do you learn new skills? How do you find answers to hard problems? What do you imagine are the best and worst parts of the job? Think through as many things as you can

Common and Contrasting Themes

In your teams, take 5 minutes to think about common themes from your answers. Also, did any of you have answers contrast with others?

Build a List of Questions

For the final 10 minutes, come back together as a large group. Each team should report common themes and interesting contrasts.

Make a list of questions that the group wants to ask people who do programming for a living.
